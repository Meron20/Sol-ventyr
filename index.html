Maldives

The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort is proud to announce that it has been awarded the prestigious Green Globe Certificate, world's leading certification for sustainable operation and management of travel and tourism worldwide. Our secluded Maldives luxury resort is only accessible via seaplane

Paris

Visit one of Paris's most famous landmarks the Eiffel Tower—with this hassle-free guided tour. You can choose from different packages—such as one that gives access to just the second level, or another that goes higher, with summit access. After meeting your guide, learn about the history of the tower, then ascend together for majestic views of the city.

Phuket

The largest and most popular island in Thailand, Phuket is renowned for some of the world's most beautiful beaches that all boast fine white sands, palm trees, crystal-clear waters, and just the right amount of nightlife.

Safari

We are a Swedish-owned family company with our own office in Nairobi and offer safaris throughout Africa, but we are experts in Kenya, Tanzania and Zanzibar. With years of experience, we know where to find these incredible animals and how to approach them respectfully.
