About Us

Sol & Äventyr AB was founded in 2005 in Stockholm and specializes in offering affordable and comfortable charter trips. With destinations in Europe, Asia and the Caribbean. The company focuses on creating simple and safe holiday experiences for families, couples and group.

FAQS

How do travel agents get paid, and will it cost me extra?

Great question! Travel agents are typically paid by the resorts, so we don't charge a service fee for our expertise. Many resorts follow price parity, meaning the cost is the same whether you book through us, directly with the resort, or via an online booking site.

What booking services does your company offer?

Finding the right flight online can be pretty challenging, all those airport codes, stopovers, and layovers. Let our flight specialists help you. We have access to some very affordable prices and can book the perfect connection that best suits your trip. Please call or live chat us and our sales team will be happy to assist with booking your flights.

Do I nedd travel insurance?

Yes. We highly recommend travel protection, it's important to protect your trip investment and loved ones. We will offer you the best option for your vacation.

Do you book group travel?

Yes, we book group travel all the time! We specialize in helping groups of all sizes plan their dream trips. From family reunions to corporate retreats, we can take care of all the details so you can relax and enjoy your time away.
